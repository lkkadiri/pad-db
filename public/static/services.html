ACID WASHES

"GREEN TO CLEAN"

Weather your pool looks like a black lagoon or you just want a fresh looking water appearance A acid wash can be used to remove scale,calcium, dirt stains,rust stains and black algae. The acid-wash process essentially strips the top layer of plaster to reveal fresh plaster beneath, so it's not recommended to do it often.



CHLORINE WASH

A chlorine wash is not only needed when your pool is green and full of algae. A chlorine wash is also a preventative measure for the good health of your in-ground swimming pool. In this process we drain the pool water into the sewer, remove all algae from pool then using high grade "non diluted" Chlorine solution we sanitize and kill off all algae and bacteria in the pool and spa.
